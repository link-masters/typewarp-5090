# ROT13 Translator Online

Encode and decode text with the ROT13 cipher instantly. Perfect for spoiler tags, internet puzzles, and exploring classic substitution ciphers. Free online.

## How ROT13 Works

ROT13 is a Caesar cipher with a fixed shift of 13. Because the English alphabet has 26 letters, applying ROT13 twice returns the original — it's its own inverse. Popularized on Usenet in the 1980s for hiding spoilers.

This tool applies ROT13 encoding. It's a simple, reversible text transformation with no cryptographic value.
## How Encoding Works

All encoding and decoding runs locally in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to any server. The output is standard text that can be copied and pasted anywhere.
