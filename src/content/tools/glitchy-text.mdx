# Glitchy Text Generator

Generate flickering, choppy glitchy text with digital distortion effects. Perfect for cyberpunk bios, mysterious Discord profiles, and dark aesthetics. Free.

## How Glitch Text Works

Glitch text combines combining diacritical marks for distortion with characters from obscure Unicode blocks to simulate digital corruption. The aesthetic draws from glitch art, VHS tracking errors, and data moshing.

This tool processes text entirely in your browser. Experiment with different styles and intensity levels.
## Unicode Blocks Used

This tool uses **Combining Diacritical Marks** (Unicode range U+0300–U+036F). These are zero-width characters that stack above, below, or through a base character. By layering multiple combining marks per letter, the text appears distorted or "glitched." This technique is sometimes called "Zalgo text" after an internet meme.
