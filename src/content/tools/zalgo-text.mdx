# Zalgo Text Generator

Create classic dripping Zalgo text with stacked diacritics and digital horror effects. Perfect for scary bios, dark Discord profiles, and creepy posts. Free.

## How This Text Style Works

This tool creates distorted text by stacking Unicode combining diacritical marks (U+0300-U+036F) above, within, and below characters. By adding multiple marks per letter, the text appears to "bleed" beyond its normal bounds.

The effect is named after a 2004 Something Awful forum meme and gained popularity on Tumblr and Discord. All processing runs in your browser — your text never leaves your device.

## Example

**Input:** `Hello`  
**Output:** `H̷̨e̶̢l̵̡l̸̢o̷̢`

## Unicode Blocks Used

This tool uses **Combining Diacritical Marks** (Unicode range U+0300–U+036F). These are zero-width characters that stack above, below, or through a base character. By layering multiple combining marks per letter, the text appears distorted or "glitched." This technique is sometimes called "Zalgo text" after an internet meme.
