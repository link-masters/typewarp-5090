# Slash Through Text Generator Online

Add a slash-through strikethrough effect to your text using Unicode. Perfect for corrections, crossed-out content, and creative social bios. Free online.

## How Slash Through Text Works

This tool combines two Unicode combining marks: **Combining Short Solidus Overlay** (U+0337) and **Combining Long Solidus Overlay** (U+0338). These zero-width characters draw horizontal or diagonal slashes through the preceding character.

The short stroke variant (U+0337) produces a compact, subtle mark, while the long solidus (U+0338) creates a full diagonal cross-out. Some implementations also use **Combining Short Stroke Overlay** (U+0335) for a traditional horizontal line.

**Unicode source:** Combining Diacritical Marks block (U+0300–U+036F). All processing runs locally in your browser.
