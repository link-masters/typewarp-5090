# Leet Speak Generator (L33T)

Convert text to classic l33tspeak with hacker-style number and symbol substitutions. Perfect for gaming handles, coder bios, and retro internet culture. Free.

## How Leetspeak (1337) Works

Leetspeak substitutes letters with visually similar numbers and symbols: A→4, E→3, L→1, T→7. Originating from 1980s BBS culture, it was used to evade text filters and as in-group signaling.

This tool converts standard text to leetspeak. Substitutions may vary — leetspeak has no formal standard.

**Input:** `Hello`  
**Output:** `H3ll0`
