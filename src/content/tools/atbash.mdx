# Atbash Cipher Generator Online

Encode and decode text using the ancient Atbash cipher instantly. Great for puzzles, secret messages, and cryptography learning. Free online tool, no signup.

## How Atbash Works

Atbash reverses the alphabet: A↔Z, B↔Y, C↔X. Originally used with the Hebrew alphabet, it's mentioned in the Book of Jeremiah. It's a simple substitution cipher with no modern security value.

This tool applies Atbash encoding to your text, running entirely in your browser.
## How Encoding Works

All encoding and decoding runs locally in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to any server. The output is standard text that can be copied and pasted anywhere.
