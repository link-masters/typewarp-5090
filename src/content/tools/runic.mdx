# Runic Translator Online

Convert text into ancient Norse Elder Futhark runes using Unicode. Perfect for mystical bios, Viking-themed profiles, and fantasy world-building. Free online.

## About Runic Transliteration

Runes are letters from Germanic alphabets used c. 150-1100 CE. The Elder Futhark contains 24 runes. This tool maps English letters to visually similar runic characters from Unicode's Runic block (U+16A0-U+16FF).

Note: This is transliteration, not translation — it maps letters, not meaning. It does not produce historically accurate Old Norse.

**Unicode source:** Runic block (U+16A0–U+16FF). All processing runs locally in your browser.

## Example

**Input:** `Hello World`  
**Output:** `ᚺᛖᛚᛚᛟ ᚹᛟᚱᛚᛞ`
